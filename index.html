Card Condition Pricing Guide

Market prices vary based on card condition. Here's how we calculate values:

Mint

100%

Perfect condition. Sharp corners, pristine surface, perfect centering. No visible wear.

Near Mint

90%

Barely played. Minimal wear, sharp corners, slight centering issues possible.

Excellent

80%

Light play wear. Minor creasing, slight corner wear, clean surface.

Very Good

70%

Moderate wear. Visible creases, corner wear, minor surface marks.

Good

60%

Heavy wear. Significant creasing, corner damage, visible surface wear.

Fair

40%

Substantial damage. Major creases, severe corner wear, fading visible.

Poor

20%

Heavily damaged. Severe creasing, tears possible, significant fading.

How Pricing Works

Our AI analyzes your card's condition and applies the appropriate multiplier to market base prices. For example:

Card Market Value: $100

Detected Condition: Near Mint (90%)

Your Card Value: $100 × 0.90 = $90
